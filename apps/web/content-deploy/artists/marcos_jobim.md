## Biography
Marcos Jobim is a Brazilian composer, singer, and multi-instrumentalist from Porto Alegre who has carved out a distinctive career blending traditional Brazilian rhythms with contemporary musical sensibilities. His musical journey began as part of the duo Cruz & Jobim, establishing himself within Brazil's vibrant music scene. Throughout his career, Marcos Jobim has developed a unique artistic voice that merges Carioca rhythms with sophisticated vocal arrangements and instrumental work, creating music that draws comparisons to the warmth and complexity of Rio's finest musical traditions.
