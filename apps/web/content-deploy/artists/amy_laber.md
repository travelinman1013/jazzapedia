## Biography
Amy Laber is a longtime New Paltz-based troubadour and musician known for her music that emphasizes the power of simplicity, the depth of country soul, and the magic of an open heart. A resident of Gardiner in New York's Hudson Valley, she has built a multifaceted career as a performer, early childhood music teacher (known as 'Miss Amy'), yoga instructor, and book-lover. For over 20 years, she has taught music classes throughout the Hudson Valley, holding relevant qualifications in early childhood education.[1][2][3][4]
