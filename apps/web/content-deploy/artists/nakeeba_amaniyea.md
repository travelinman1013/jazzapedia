## Biography
Nakeeba Amaniyea is a DJ, rapper, and performer in the reggae and dancehall traditions, best known as the daughter of legendary reggae artist Sister Carol (Carol Theresa East). Born and raised with early exposure to the music industry through her mother's career, Nakeeba was immersed in Jamaica's vibrant reggae scene from a young age, with her family roots in Kingston, Jamaica. This background intrigued and enhanced her musical artistry, leading her to follow in her mother's footsteps as a deejay while developing her own style blending R&B vocals, rap, ska, and conscious dancehall flows.[1][2][6]
