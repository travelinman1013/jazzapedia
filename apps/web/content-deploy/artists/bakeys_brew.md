## Biography
Bakey's Brew is a progressive psych-rock band based in New Orleans, Louisiana, known for their technical compositions and immersive soundscapes.[4] The five-piece ensemble creates music that moves effortlessly between grooves, psychedelic textures, and heavy riffs, with live performances often described as an auditory journey offering a seamless blend of energy, depth, and originality.[4] The band's name is an acronym using the last names of all five original members, with 'Brew' as a nod to Will Brown's (lead guitarist) career as a master brewer and their unique blend of sounds.[3]

Bakey's Brew plays original music and has established themselves within the New Orleans music scene through regular performances and festival appearances.[5] They hold down a regular Monday night residency at the famed Maple Leaf Bar on Oak Street.[3] The band was recognized by the Threadhead Cultural Foundation, receiving grants that acknowledged their contributions to the local music community.[3] Their sound represents a contemporary approach to progressive rock, blending technical proficiency with psychedelic and groove-oriented elements that reflect the diverse musical heritage of New Orleans.
