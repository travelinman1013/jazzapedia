## Biography
Walter Miller is an emerging pop-rock singer-songwriter from the Bayou region of the United States, known for his bold blend of classic rock bravado from the '70s and '80s with modern pop polish. He has evolved from initial insecurities about his identity and stage presence to embracing unapologetic self-expression, including being openly gay, which infuses his music with personal confidence and theatrical energy. His latest single, 'Basic Instinct,' marks a defining moment, inspired by wearing thigh-high boots and treating each album cycle as potentially his last, pushing him to create authentic rock music that reflects his true artistic vision[3][7].

Stylistically, he pays homage to stadium rock legends while updating the genre for contemporary audiences, aiming to inspire listeners to explore rock's foundations and create their own music[3].
