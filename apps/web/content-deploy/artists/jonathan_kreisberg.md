## Biography
Jonathan Kreisberg was born in New York City in 1972, and his family relocated to Miami when he was a young child. Exposed to diverse music from his parents, he began playing guitar at age 10, studying music theory, rock, and classical guitar. At 16, he entered the New World School of the Arts in Miami, focusing on jazz, and gained early recognition with features in Guitar Player and DownBeat magazines while still in high school. He earned a scholarship to the University of Miami Jazz Program, graduating in 1994, where he held the guitar chair in the Concert Jazz Band and performed with Joe Henderson, Michael Brecker, and Red Rodney.[1][2][3][4]

## Fun Facts
- Performed with the New World Symphony under Michael Tilson Thomas on 20th-century works and opened for rock legends Steve Vai and Yngwie Malmsteen with progressive rock group Third Wish.[3]
- Self-released his first trio album at age 21 right after University of Miami graduation.[4]
- Recorded solo guitar album 'One' in 2013 with liberal interpretations of standards live, no overdubs, impressing reviewers with prodigious chops.[4]
- Toured Brazil with University of Miami Concert Jazz Band and held guitar chair there.[1][3]
