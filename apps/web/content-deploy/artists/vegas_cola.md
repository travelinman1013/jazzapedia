## Biography
Vegas Cola is a singer, songwriter, and bandleader from New Orleans, who grew up in the historic Treme neighborhood, one of the city's most musical areas. She has captivated audiences in the Crescent City for over 20 years, establishing herself as one of New Orleans' most well-known best-kept secrets in R&B, soul, funk, jazz, and bounce music. Her career highlights include her debut at the French Quarter Festival and contributions to the local scene through performances and songwriting.[2][5][6]
