## Biography
Joshua Spence Mainnie, known professionally as Barry Can't Swim, was born on 22 August 1992 in Edinburgh, Scotland. A classically trained pianist from age 9, he developed a deep love for jazz through teenage years working in jazz bars, which shaped his musical foundation. During university, he expanded his skills by playing guitar, bass, and drums in various bands and interned at Glasgow's SOMA Records, gaining insight into the underground electronic scene.[1][2][3][4]
