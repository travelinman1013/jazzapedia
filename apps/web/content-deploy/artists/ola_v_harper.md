# Ola V HArper

## Biography
Ola V. Harper was an R&B and soul vocalist who recorded during the late 1960s. She is known for her work in the soul and R&B genre, with her recordings released on independent labels during the height of the soul music era. Her most notable recording, 'Resisting,' was released on Jewel Records (catalog #797) in 1969 and is characterized as a 'wicked female r&b soul 45 rocker' with production by Peppermint Harris. The song showcases her vocal delivery on themes of romantic devotion and emotional vulnerability, with lyrics emphasizing the irresistibility of love. While detailed biographical information about her early life and career trajectory remains limited in readily available sources, her work represents the broader landscape of independent soul and R&B recordings from the late 1960s.
