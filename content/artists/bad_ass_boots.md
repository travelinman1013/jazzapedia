## Biography
Bad Ass Boots is an Americana and country-rock band originating from the San Francisco Bay Area, specifically Oakland, California.[2][4][8] The ensemble consists of five members—four women and one man—who create a distinctive hybrid sound blending traditional country with modern sensibilities.[2][4][8] Their musical approach emphasizes original compositions that are described as sensual, tough, and fun-loving, reflecting their unique artistic vision within the Americana genre.[4][6]
